Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and contact the WasteZero team if you’re stuck.

Getting started
  • Users: Go to Schedule Pickup to request a new pickup, then track it under My Pickups and your impact under My Impact.
  • Volunteers: Browse and accept opportunities under Opportunities, then manage your work from My Applications and My Pickups.
  • Admins: Use the Admin Panel to monitor stats, manage users, pickups, and export reports.
Messaging & notifications
  • Use the Messages page to chat with users, volunteers, or admins about pickups and opportunities.
  • Unread messages and important events (like accepted pickups or applications) appear in the notification bell at the top-right.
  • If you don’t see new messages, check that your browser allows WebSocket connections and that you have a stable network.
FAQs

How do I reset my password?
Contact an admin or your organization’s support team to reset credentials.

Why can’t I see some pages?
Access depends on your role (user, volunteer, admin). Ask an admin if you need elevated access.

Pickup not updating?
Refresh the page, then check Messages or contact support with your pickup ID.
